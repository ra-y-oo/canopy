Our Story

Founded in early 2025, GreenEarth emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to combat climate change one tree at a time. What began as a small community initiative has blossomed into a nationwide movement, bringing together individuals, families, schools, and corporations in a shared mission to restore our planet's green cover.

Our foundation believes in the power of small actions creating big impacts. Each tree planted not only helps absorb CO₂ but also provides habitat for wildlife, prevents soil erosion, and creates lasting environmental benefits for generations to come.

With a dedicated team of environmental experts, community organizers, and passionate volunteers, we're working tirelessly to reach our goal of planting 1,000 trees by 2026. But we're not just planting trees – we're growing a community of environmental stewards committed to protecting our planet's future.

Why Trees? Why Now?

Climate Change Mitigation

Trees are natural carbon capture machines, absorbing CO₂ and releasing oxygen. A single mature tree can absorb up to 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year.

Biodiversity Protection

Trees provide essential habitats for countless species. A single oak tree can support over 500 different species of wildlife and insects.

Water Conservation

Trees help prevent soil erosion, reduce water pollution, and regulate the water cycle. Their root systems act as natural water filters.

Community Well-being

Urban trees reduce stress, improve air quality, and create spaces for community connection. They can increase property values by up to 15%.
